The Story.

GolfOptimise was motivated by my passion for tracking golf analytics to help improve my own game. The current options on the market to keep track of meaningful insights - particularly stroked gained data benchmarked against the PGA Tour Average - cost hundreds of pounds per year to subscribe to (a nonnecessity I did not have the money for). I have been using the dashboard since 2023 and used the insights gained from it to win my first Club Championship in June 2024 as well as getting to a County championship Final a month later.

I was first introduced to the advantage you can gain over competitors by applying a "Moneyball" approach to your own golf game during my golf scholarship at the University of St Andrews. The University kindly paid our subscription to one of the analytics tracking platforms available. This cost hundreds of pounds.

When I left university, I could no longer afford to fund this myself so I used my coding skills to develop a much more simplified version of the dashboard I offer today. In the past few months, I have decided to enhance the user interface and deepen the insights the dashboard offers for all golfing abilities, so that all golfers can improve without having to spend countless hours grinding balls on the driving range.

I have made this available at a very low price which just about covers the hosting costs of the website as I believe you should be able to benefit from turning your shots on the course to meaningful insights for a low cost.

James Conn

+2 Handicap, Data Analyst & Full Stack Developer
